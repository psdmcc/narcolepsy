# Clinical Study Protocol: N-of-1 Longitudinal Evaluation of an Alternative Multi-Modal Metabolic Protocol for Severe Narcolepsy Type 1 (NT1)

**Principal Investigator**: Patrick S.D. McCartney
**Affiliation**: Graduate School of Humanities and Social Sciences, Hiroshima University

## 1. Introduction & Background
Narcolepsy Type 1 (NT1) is characterized by the autoimmune destruction of ~70,000 orexinergic neurons in the lateral hypothalamus. Current synthetic standards of care (modafinil, oxybates) manage symptoms by manipulating downstream monoaminergic pathways but fail to address the underlying neuroinflammatory axis or cell-degeneration cascade. This study tracks a novel protocol combining low-dose monoaminergic reuptake inhibition (100mg Modafinil) with multi-modal alternative modifiers (free-radical scavengers, metal chelators, immunomodulators) to optimize therapeutic sensitivity and reduce systemic symptom expression.

## 2. Study Design & Timeline Metrics
This project utilizes an open-label, longitudinal N-of-1 single-subject design divided into distinct operational blocks over a 90-day observation window:

* **Days 1–30 (Baseline Optimization Phase)**: Maintenance of the fully established protocol (100mg Modafinil morning window + full alternative stack) to document current functional stability.
* **Days 31–45 (Targeted Washout Phase - Variable Isolation)**: Systematic removal of ONE independent variable (e.g., oral EDTA chelation) while keeping all other parameters constant. This phase tests the specific therapeutic weight of that isolated modifier.
* **Days 46–90 (Re-Introduction & Final Maintenance Phase)**: Re-introduction of the isolated variable to verify if symptom reduction returns to optimal baseline metrics.

## 3. Measurable Variables & Endpoints

### A. Primary Objective Endpoints (Laboratory Biomarkers)
Blood draws will be conducted at a local clinic at Day 1, Day 45, and Day 90 to monitor these specific safety and efficacy markers:
1. **Neuroinflammatory Markers**: High-Sensitivity C-Reactive Protein (hs-CRP) and Interleukin-6 (IL-6).
2. **Hepatic Clearance Safety**: ALT, AST, Total Bilirubin, and GGT.
3. **Renal Filtration Safety**: Serum Creatinine and eGFR.
4. **Trace Mineral System Balance**: Serum Zinc and Serum Copper ratios.

### B. Secondary Functional Endpoints (Subjective Rating Scales)
The subject will complete standardized clinical assessments weekly:
1. **Epworth Sleepiness Scale (ESS)**: To measure daytime sleep propensity.
2. **Stanford Sleepiness Scale (SSS)**: Completed 3x daily (09:00, 14:00, 20:00) to map diurnal wakefulness paths.
3. **Symptom Tracker**: Quantitative log of daily REM intrusions (sleep attacks) and cataplexy events.

import os
import csv
from datetime import datetime

def run_clinical_trial_registry(output_dir="data_payloads"):
    """
    Automates data collection for an N-of-1 self-experimentation case study,
    formatting entries cleanly to prepare for medical journal submission.
    """
    os.makedirs(output_dir, exist_ok=True)
    csv_path = os.path.join(output_dir, "clinical_trial_registry.csv")
    
    # Establish standard clinical columns
    headers = [
        "Date", "Study_Phase", "Modafinil_Dose_mg", "Stanford_Sleepiness_Scale_Avg",
        "Sleep_Attacks_Count", "Nocturnal_Sleep_Quality_1to10", "Adverse_Events_Logged", "Notes"
    ]
    
    file_exists = os.path.exists(csv_path)
    
    print("=== CLINICAL TRIAL REGISTRY: N-OF-1 NARCOLEPSY PROJECT ===")
    print("Record your daily empirical metrics below:\n")
    
    


# Gather your clinical parameters via terminal prompts
    phase = input("Current Study Phase (e.g., Baseline, EDTA-Washout, Maintenance): ") or "Baseline"
    modafinil = input("Modafinil Dose (mg) [e.g., 100]: ") or "100"
    sss_score = input("Stanford Sleepiness Scale Daily Average (1=Fully Active, 7=Severe Sleepiness): ") or "1"
    attacks = input("Daytime REM Intrusions / Sleep Attacks [Count]: ") or "0"
    sleep_quality = input("Nocturnal Sleep Consolidation Score [1-10]: ") or "10"
    adverse = input("Adverse Events Logged (e.g., skin rash, heart palpitations, None): ") or "None"
    notes = input("Daily observations: ") or "Protocol parameters stable."
    
    row_data = {
        "Date": datetime.now().strftime("%Y-%m-%d"),
        "Study_Phase": phase,
        "Modafinil_Dose_mg": modafinil,
        "Stanford_Sleepiness_Scale_Avg": sss_score,
        "Sleep_Attacks_Count": attacks,
        "Nocturnal_Sleep_Quality_1to10": sleep_quality,
        "Adverse_Events_Logged": adverse,
        "Notes": notes
    }
    
    with open(csv_path, "a", encoding="utf-8", newline="") as f:
        writer = csv.DictWriter(f, fieldnames=headers)
        if not file_exists:
            writer.writeheader()
        writer.writerow(row_data)
        
    print(f"\nSuccess! Entry appended cleanly to your clinical database.")
    print(f"File Location: {csv_path}")

if __name__ == "__main__":
    run_clinical_trial_registry()
